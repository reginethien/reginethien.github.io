First of all, what is AI?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It is the science of teaching machines to mimic intelligent human behaviors. AI systems are taught using computer programming to learn, reason, problem solve, and create!

Hm....So then what is GENERATIVE AI?

Gen AI is a leap forward from traditional AI, which was focused on understanding and processing information. Gen AI are computer programs that use artificial intelligence to create new content. Gen AI can also find existing information on the web, but its main use is to create content. Some examples of Gen AI platforms are ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews and Midjourney.

Lesson 3: The Good...and The Bad

While Gen AI offers numerous benefits, it also presents several risks and challenges.

The Good - Gen AI provides benefits such as time-saving capabilities for repetitive tasks or research, and digital assistance.

The Bad - If not careful, using Gen AI may not keep your personal information safe, could cause copyright issues, or spread misinformation.

Lesson 3: The Good...and The Bad

Here are some risks that come with the misuse of Gen AI

Video Deepfake - Realistic videos made by face swapping, used for entertainment or spreading misinformation

Audio Deepfake - Voice recordings created to mimic someone’s voice to deceive

Text Plagiarism - Creating text to imitate someone’s writing style, but posing it as your own

Lesson 3: The Good...and The Bad

How do we prevent AI misuse?

We should define the purpose, scope, and limits of your AI project; follow AI ethical principles for its design and development. We should not forget to conduct risk assessments for our AI project, and implement controls to mitigate our AI misuse!
